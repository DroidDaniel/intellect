Our Story

PVM Herbal Products

Panavelil Vaidyamadom Herbals is a product of the decades of ancient Ayurvedic knowledge bequeathed from generations. PVM Herbals believes in the primeval science of healing and in using an ideal meld of Ayurveda together with modern sciences and formulations to create a proprietary blend.

The Vaidya Chikitsa (ayurvedic treatments) was a part of Panavelil Vaidyamadom’s tradition. Eventually, it evoked a thought that the world should recognize the power of ayurvedic healing and they consequently felt the need to extend their knowledge beyond their vaidya sala (treatment center) and bring the traditional Indian science of Ayurveda to society in a contemporary form. We are evolving as a head-to-toe wellness provider by keeping the purity of the ingredients and efficacy of the formulations at the heart of our products. We use naturally-derived ingredients combined with naturally occurring carrier agents, surfactants, humectants, and emulsifiers, etc.

to bring you the most high quality and effectual products. At PVM we believe that health should be a keystone habit. In our Contemporary society, health is misinterpreted to be a repercussion of Medicines & Hospitals. People are so addicted to pharmacological treatments that they get exploited by the big pharmaceuticals & hospitals.

Once you recognize how amazing the human body is, you wouldn't want to continue to take your very own for granted. Keeping that in mind, we manufacture our products in a way to preserve the integrity of the ingredients so that you are committed to wellness. Thereupon our motive is to bring you the magic of nature and its therapeutic effects. It’s a commitment to the long game of true health and wellness. In our words, we would like to help you embrace nature and thus... embrace health!
